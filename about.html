FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle? +

You need a valid government-issued ID (Aadhaar, Passport, or Voter ID) and a valid driving license. For bike rentals, a valid two-wheeler license is mandatory.

Is there a security deposit? +

No! RideEasy charges zero security deposit. You only pay the rental amount plus a 10% service fee and ₹50 insurance charge per booking.

What is your cancellation policy? +

You can cancel your booking for free up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Cancellations within 2 hours are non-refundable. No-shows will be charged the full booking amount.

Are the vehicles insured? +

Yes! Every vehicle comes with comprehensive third-party insurance. The ₹50 insurance fee in your booking covers you for the full rental period. We also include 24/7 roadside assistance.

Can I extend my rental period? +

Absolutely! Call our support line (1800-RIDEEASY) or contact us via WhatsApp at least 1 hour before your return time, and we'll extend your booking subject to vehicle availability.

Do you offer doorstep delivery? +

Yes, doorstep delivery is available in select areas for an additional fee of ₹99-₹199 depending on the distance. You can mention your delivery address during booking.
