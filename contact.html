FAQ

Common Questions

Can't find your answer? Reach out to us directly.

How do I track my booking? +

After booking, you'll receive an SMS and email with your booking ID. You can use this ID to track your booking status by contacting our support team at 1800-RIDEEASY.

What if the vehicle breaks down? +

We provide 24/7 roadside assistance. In case of a breakdown, call our helpline and our team will reach you within 30-60 minutes. If the vehicle can't be fixed on-site, we'll arrange a replacement.

Can I rent vehicles for multiple days? +

Absolutely! You can rent for as little as 1 hour or as long as 30 days. Multi-day rentals (3+ days) receive a 15% discount on the base price automatically.

Is there an age requirement? +

Yes. You must be at least 18 years old and hold a valid two-wheeler driving license. For bikes above 350cc, you must be 21+ years and have held your license for at least 1 year.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept cash at pickup, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and Net Banking from all major Indian banks.
